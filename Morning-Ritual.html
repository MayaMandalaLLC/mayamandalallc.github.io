MORNING RITUALS

A simple, mindful oral care routine to help you begin the day feeling fresh, clean and centered.

Your Ritual

☼ ♧ ╱

1. Brush Gently

Use a charcoal toothbrush with herbal toothpaste and brush gently in small circular motions. Rinse thoroughly after brushing.

2. Cleanse the Tongue

Hold the copper tongue cleaner at both ends, place it gently toward the back of the tongue, and pull forward with light pressure. Rinse between passes.

♨ ♧

3. Optional Oil Pulling

Oil pulling is a traditional self-care practice. Swish gently, discard the oil in a trash bin, and rinse well. It is not a substitute for brushing, flossing, or regular dental care.

♧

Gentle Use Note

Use gently and discontinue use if irritation occurs. Consult your dentist or healthcare professional if you have oral discomfort or dental concerns. Maya Mandala products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
